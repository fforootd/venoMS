Welcome to venoMS

venoMS is the database for low molecular weight compounds (< 1000 Da) in spider venoms. providing general compound information, LC-MS, MS/MS and HDX data for the identification of known compounds and links to the compound related literature regarding structure elucidation from spider venom, synthesis and biological activity. Although our focus is on structure elucidation, this database is intended to be useful for those generally interested in spider venoms or polyamine toxins.
